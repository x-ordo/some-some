## Screenshot Style Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Background:** Dark theme (#17171C)
- **Accent Color:** Kitsch Pink (#FF007F)
- **Typography:** Clean, large text overlays

### Device Frames
- Use device mockups for professional look
- iPhone 15 Pro / Pixel 8 Pro recommended
- Optional: angled or perspective views

### Text Overlays
- Max 5 words per caption
- High contrast (white on dark)
- Position at top or bottom
- Use app's typography style

### Composition Tips
1. Show actual gameplay, not placeholder graphics
2. Highlight the two-player aspect (two fingers visible)
3. Emphasize the romantic/fun atmosphere
4. Include confetti/celebration in success screen

## App Icon Requirements

### Google Play
- **Size:** 512 x 512 px
- **Format:** 32-bit PNG (with alpha)
- **Shape:** Full bleed, system applies mask

### App Store
- **Size:** 1024 x 1024 px
- **Format:** PNG (no transparency, no rounded corners)
- **Shape:** Square, system applies mask
